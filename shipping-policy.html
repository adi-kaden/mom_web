Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Antiqua Gallery, we understand that our pieces are not just purchases but treasured investments. We take the utmost care in packaging and shipping every item to ensure it arrives safely at your destination.

White Glove Service: All shipments include our signature white glove delivery service with full insurance coverage.

Domestic Shipping (Kazakhstan)

We offer secure delivery throughout Kazakhstan:

  • Almaty City: Personal delivery available, typically within 1-3 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-10 business days via secure courier service
  • All domestic shipments are fully insured at no additional cost

International Shipping

We ship our pieces worldwide with experienced fine art shipping partners:

  • CIS Countries: 7-14 business days
  • Europe: 10-21 business days
  • Asia & Middle East: 14-28 business days
  • Americas: 14-30 business days

International shipping costs and timelines vary based on destination, item size, and required customs documentation. A detailed quote will be provided before purchase confirmation.

Packaging Standards

Every piece is professionally packed using museum-quality materials:

  • Acid-free tissue and padding materials
  • Custom-fitted wooden crates for fragile items
  • Climate-controlled containers when necessary
  • Tamper-evident seals and tracking

Insurance

All shipments are fully insured for the complete purchase value. Our insurance coverage includes:

  • Damage during transit
  • Loss or theft
  • Natural disasters during shipping

Customs and Import Duties

For international orders:

  • Buyers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and import fees
  • We provide all necessary documentation for customs clearance
  • Certificate of authenticity and provenance papers accompany every shipment
  • We can assist with cultural export permits when required
